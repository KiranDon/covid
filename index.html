About COVID-19.

What is Corona virus?

The coronavirus is a family of viruses that can cause a range of illnesses in humans including common cold and more severe forms like SARS and MERS which are life-threatening. The virus is named after its shape which takes the form of a crown with protrusions around it and hence is known as coronavirus.

Corona Virus Symptoms

Cough

Cold

Fever

Sour Throat

Difficult breathing

Tiredness

Steps to prevent the spread of virus.

Wash your hands toroughly for atleast 20 seconds frequently.

Maintain Social distancing.(Atleast 1 Meter)

Always wear a mask as the main source of spreading is by air.

Stay Home, Stay Safe. Dont go outside un-necessarily.

Stay informed about the pandemic by following official sources.

Seek medical consultation if you have any of these symptoms.
